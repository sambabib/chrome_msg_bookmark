Privacy Policy for Pinner

Last Updated: July 2, 2025

Introduction

Pinner ("we," "our," or "this extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data when using our browser extension.

Information Collection

What We Collect

Pinner collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Bookmark Content: Text that you explicitly select and choose to bookmark
  • Timestamp: When each bookmark was created
  • Platform Information: Whether the bookmark was created on Claude or ChatGPT
  • Message ID: A reference to identify which message in a conversation contains the bookmarked text

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or transmit any personal information
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not access any data from websites other than Claude and ChatGPT
  • We do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies
  • We do not have a backend server that receives your data

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Display your bookmarks in the extension popup
  • Navigate to the exact location of bookmarked text when requested
  • Allow you to search and filter your saved bookmarks

Data Storage

All data collected by Pinner is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's storage.local API. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Your data is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Your data is only accessible to you within your browser
  • Your data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the extension

Permissions

Pinner requires certain permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab's content for detecting selections and highlighting bookmarks
  • storage: To save your bookmarks locally on your device
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options for bookmarking
  • Host Permissions (for Claude and ChatGPT domains): To inject the necessary scripts that enable bookmarking functionality

These permissions are used solely for providing the core functionality of the extension and not for collecting or transmitting data.

Third-Party Access

Pinner does not share any data with third parties. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

Data Retention

Your bookmarks are retained in your browser's local storage until:

  • You manually delete them using the extension's interface
  • You clear your browser's extension storage
  • You uninstall the extension

Children's Privacy

Pinner is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension's documentation and updating the "Last Updated" date.

By using Pinner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
